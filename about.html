Our Story

In 2010, Ghulam Mustafa Ismail started a small workshop in Wapda Town, Multan, with a simple mission: to provide quality engine oils and reliable auto electrical & AC services to the local community.

Ghulam Mustafa personally ensured every vehicle received the best care, building strong relationships with customers who returned again and again.

Over the years, Al-Makka Autos & Auto Mobile Oil became known for stocking top brands like Havoline, Total Quattro, Shell Helix, and SKZ, and for expert car AC repair and auto electrical services. Today, we are also proud authorized dealers of high-performance Alaska and Al-Saika batteries.
